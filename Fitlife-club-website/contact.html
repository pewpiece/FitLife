Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring for my first visit?

Bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide all necessary equipment for your workout.

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new members. This includes access to all facilities and one complimentary personal training session.

What are your cancellation policies?

You can cancel your membership with 30 days' notice. We also offer membership freezes for up to 3 months for medical or travel reasons.

Do you have childcare services?

We offer supervised childcare during peak hours (9 AM - 12 PM and 5 PM - 8 PM) for children ages 6 months to 12 years.

Are personal trainers included?

Personal training sessions are included with Premium and Elite memberships. Basic members can book sessions at discounted rates.

What safety measures do you have?

We maintain strict cleanliness protocols, have certified staff on-site, emergency procedures, and all equipment is regularly inspected and maintained.
